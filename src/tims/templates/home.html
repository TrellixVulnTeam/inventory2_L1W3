Computer Inventory Management

Computer inventory management is the process of automatically discovering, tracking, and centrally managing hardware IT assets. It helps you keep your IT inventory up to date and allows you to track assets throughout their life cycle.

Track Assets

The first step to gaining control of your Computer inventory is establishing an up-to-date list of your hardware and software assets. Manually tracking IT assets on spreadsheets is time-consuming and prone to errors
